Biography:

Hong Xie is currently a researcher at the Big Data Research Center, Chongqing Institute of Green and Intelligent Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He received Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2015, proudly under the supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui. He received his B.Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at The University of Science and Technology of China in 2010. Hong Xie was a postdoctoral research fellow at Department of Computing Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) hosted by Prof. John C.S. Lui, and a postdoctoral research fellow at School of Computing, Nationial University of Singapore hosted by Prof. Richard T.B. Ma . He was also a faculty member at Chongqing University. He is a member of CCF, a member of IEEE and a member of ACM.

Research

  • Research interests: DATA MINING, MACHINE LEARNING
    Particularly focus on desgning and analyzing data driven decision making models and algorithms for Internet applications such as recommendation systems, online social networks, cloud systems, etc. Currently, I am very interested in data driven decision making problems arising from AIOps (artificial intelligence for IT operations).
