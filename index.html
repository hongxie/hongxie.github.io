Biography:

Hong Xie is currently a research professor at School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) . He is a member of Prof. Enhong Chen's research group. He received Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2015, proudly under the supervision of Prof. John C.S. Lui. He received his B.Eng. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology at USTC in 2010. Hong Xie was a postdoctoral research fellow at Department of Computing Science and Engineering, CUHK hosted by Prof. John C.S. Lui, and a postdoctoral research fellow at School of Computing, Nationial University of Singapore hosted by Prof. Richard T.B. Ma . He was also a faculty member at Chongqing University. He is a member of CCF, a member of IEEE and a member of ACM.

Research

  • Research interests: Context aware online learning including contextual bandits and contextual reinforcement learning, with a special focus on the large scale setting.
